Who Are We

Dan Avila & Sons is a shipper of fresh produce located in the agricultural rich San Joaquin Valley of central California, lies the quaint farm town of Turlock that we call home. It is here and close surrounding areas where we cultivate our crops just as our forefathers and their families did before us, dating back to 1916. Today, we carry on with the legacy they left behind; to produce the finest watermelons and sweet potatoes as humanly possible as we can.
